About Hempstead

The Village of Hempstead is the largest village in New York State and the seat of the Town of Hempstead. Anchored by Hofstra University and Nassau Community College, it has a dense commercial corridor along Fulton Avenue and Main Street, plus retail and auto-service along Hempstead Turnpike and Peninsula Boulevard. The mix of student housing, multi-family residential, places of worship, and small storefront commerce makes inspection coverage especially varied here.

What we typically service in Hempstead

We commonly service Hempstead restaurants and food trucks (Class K for fryers, ABC for dining areas), multi-family residential buildings near the Hofstra campus (common-area extinguishers, mechanical rooms, laundry rooms), retail and convenience stores along Fulton Avenue, auto-service shops, and the village’s many churches and community centers.

Services we provide in Hempstead

  • Annual inspection & tagging — NFPA 10 yearly inspection with dated certification tags and a written report.
  • Recharge & refill — ABC, CO₂, and Class K units recharged to factory specs, often same-day.
  • Hydrostatic testing — 5-year and 12-year cylinder pressure tests required by NFPA 10.
  • New extinguisher supply — UL-listed equipment sized to your hazard, mounted and tagged.
  • Compliance audits — pre-fire-marshal walkthrough, gap report, and on-the-spot fixes.
  • Multi-site management — one vendor for portfolio-wide extinguisher service across multiple Hempstead-area properties.
